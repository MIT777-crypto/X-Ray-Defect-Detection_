About Our Technology

Revolutionizing Medical Diagnostics

After completing 6 years of rigorous medical education, doctors still require significant time and resources to accurately analyze X-rays. This process is not only time-consuming but also costly for both medical professionals and patients.

MedScan AI leverages advanced deep learning algorithms to analyze X-ray images in seconds, detecting defects and abnormalities with unprecedented accuracy. Our technology provides medical professionals with a powerful diagnostic tool that saves time, reduces costs, and improves patient outcomes.

By automating the initial analysis, we allow doctors to focus on treatment planning and patient care, while ensuring that potential issues are never overlooked.

How to Use Our System

1

Upload Your X-Ray

Click the "Select File" button or drag and drop your X-ray image onto the upload area. We accept JPG, PNG, and DICOM formats.

2

AI Analysis

Our advanced AI algorithms will analyze your X-ray image, scanning for fractures, tumors, infections, and other abnormalities with 99.99% accuracy.

3

Review Results

View the analysis results which will clearly indicate if the X-ray is defective or non-defective, with highlighted areas of concern.

4

Consult a Professional

Share the results with your healthcare provider. Remember, our system is a diagnostic aid and not a replacement for professional medical advice.
